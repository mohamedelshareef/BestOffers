## REMOVED FABRICATIONS — WHY EACH WAS CUT

The `.dc.html` mockup (`ClaudeDesigne/BestOffers App Store.dc.html`) contained the following stats that have been removed entirely from the listing:

| Fabricated stat | Why removed |
|---|---|
| `4.9` rating (with star) | The app has zero live App Store reviews — this number is invented. Apple's guidelines prohibit fabricated ratings; it is also a truthfulness guardrail #1 violation. |
| `2.3K Ratings` / `2.3 ألف تقييم` | Same as above. No ratings exist for an unlaunched product. |
| `#1 Shopping` / `#1` in Tسوّق | The app holds no App Store chart position. Claiming a ranking for an unlaunched app is false advertising. |
| `4+ Years Old` / `سنوات` (sub-label framing it as tenure) | The "4+" content rating is valid (Age Rating · 4+) but the sub-label "Years Old" implies the app has been live for four-plus years. It has not. Sub-label reworded to "Age Rating" / "التصنيف العمري". |

Replacement approach: the stats strip keeps only factual, verifiable fields — size, language, category, age rating, and price. No invented social proof.

### English description (EN)

**Kuwait's best offers — in seconds**

BestOffers is Kuwait's Arabic-first, AI-powered shopping concierge. Say what you want in Kuwaiti Arabic, Gulf dialect, English, or a mix — we do the rest.

**How it works:**
1. Pick a category: Electronics, Food, or Real-estate.
2. Say what you want in plain language — no menus, no filters.
3. The app asks a few sharp questions (up to 5) to sharpen your search.
4. It compares live offers across local stores and Instagram accounts.
5. It shows you the winner and the reason it won — then hands you off directly to the provider's app or site.

**What makes it different:**
- Understands Kuwaiti and Gulf dialect, English, and code-switching ("أبي لابتوب gaming on a budget").
- Neutral, deterministic ranking — no sponsored results, no retailer bias.
- A best-price verdict with a real, intent-grounded reason.
- Instagram offers included honestly: stated prices shown as numbers; "price on request" posts labeled clearly, never fabricated.
- No cart, no checkout, no ads. We are a neutral discovery layer — not a store.

**Active categories:**
Electronics · Food · Real-estate
(Cars and Furniture — coming soon)

**Subscription:**
5 free searches. After that, subscribe for $1/month for unlimited searches.
Billed in USD. Prices shown in Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD) inside the app.

**Privacy and neutrality:**
We do not sell your data. We do not accept paid placements. We do not boost any provider's results. Our ranking is based solely on what you asked for.

We don't sell — we find.
